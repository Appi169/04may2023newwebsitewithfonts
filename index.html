India added 7 million mobile subscriptions during Q2 2023, claims report

India accounted for the most net addition of mobile subscriptions with seven million during the second quarter of 2023 (Q2 2023) that ended on August 31 even as 5G subscriptions grew by 175 million globally, a new report has said.

After India, China (five million) took the second spot in net addition of mobile subscriptions, followed by the US at three million, as per the latest Ericsson Mobility Report. It also said that the additions in the quarter bring the global number of 5G subscriptions close to 1.3 billion.

BGMI collaborates with Ranveer Singh: Watch the new teaser

Kraftons Battlegrounds Mobile India (BGMI) made a comeback in India after being banned in 2022. The survival shooter battle royale mobile title was banned in the country due to concerns surrounding data privacy and national security. Since its comeback, the game’s developer has announced several events and collaborations to woo back its massive player base. In its latest promotion, the game has announced its collaboration with a popular Bollywood actor. According to a report by IGN, this celebrity may become BGMI’s latest ambassador.
